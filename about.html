Help Me Choose !

Create fun polls and ask your family, friends and followers to Help You Choose what to buy!

To get started, drag & drop the Help Me Choose Bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar.
Help Me Choose-Bookmarklet

Spoilt for choice? Sometimes you just can't decide.

Making a wish list will only take you so far when what you really need is friendly advice.Or maybe you need to know what the crowd wants right now.

Point is: You need to ask people for their opinion while shopping online.

We'll help you choose! In a couple of clicks:

• Create a side-by-side comparison of several products from any e-commerce website

• Click on the bookmarklet and post the poll on Social Network or Email to let your friends & followers vote for their favorite product!

• Get notified about the poll results.

How to start? What is this bookmarklet?

Installing and deleting it is as simple as saving and deleting a bookmark.
